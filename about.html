About

Troy School District middle school students are invited to be a part of an engaging, fun, and free summer learning experience at Boulan Park Middle School! Students will design and develop a plan or prototype to address a problem or issue they are passionate about. Throughout the process, students will work with experts in various fields and engage in learning experiences around Troy and the larger Metro community. Fields of focus will encompass student learning within math, science, language arts, world studies, social student, fine and preforming arts and more. This program aims to empower student voice via collaboration with peers and to help students become agents of change while also providing a fun and safe self-directed learning environment.
