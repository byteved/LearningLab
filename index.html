Design Process

Step 1: Empathize

Empathy is a way to understand your audience so you can tailor your solution to meet its needs.

Step 2: Define

Define means you're working towards a purpose.

Step 3: Ideate

This is the trial and error part of the process. Zoom out and flare to create a lot of ideas and use feedback to focus on the best one(s) that you will develop.

Step 4: Prototype

Flush out your brainstormed ideas that could become a solution to your audience's problem.

Step 5: Test

Present your design to your partner to ensure that your solution is best suiting their needs. Take them through all the details of your imagined design.
